Jiao Lab

Department of Biological Sciences, National University of Singapore

Principal Investigator

Engineering CRISPR systems into precise molecular tools.

Our lab studies the discovery, engineering, and design of CRISPR and novel bacterial defense systems for diagnostics and therapeutics, with a focus on RNA detection, genome editing, RNA knock-down, and RNA recording.

Current Projects

Platforms for reading, writing, and recording RNA biology.

RNA Detection

Sensitive, high-throughput detection platforms built from reprogrammed CRISPR systems.

Knock-In

Precise, high-efficiency knock-in tools for programmable genome engineering.

RNA Knock-Down

Non-cytotoxic RNA knock-down systems designed for cleaner perturbation studies.

Genome Knock-Out

Long-range genome knock-out platforms for bacterial and cellular engineering.

RNA Recording

Spatial RNA recording platforms for tracing molecular histories inside cells.

Lab Members
